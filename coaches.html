Our Coaches

Meet the leaders who have shaped WSU Rugby throughout the years

John Smith

Years Coached: 2018-2023
Hometown: Minneapolis, MN
College: University of Minnesota

Led the team to multiple championship victories and developed innovative training programs that transformed the team's playing style. Known for his dedication to player development both on and off the field.

Mike Johnson

Years Coached: 2010-2017
Hometown: Rochester, MN
College: Winona State University

A WSU alumni who brought passion and deep understanding of the program's traditions. Focused on building team chemistry and establishing the foundation for future success.

Sarah Williams

Years Coached: 2005-2009, 2024-Present
Hometown: La Crosse, WI
College: University of Wisconsin-La Crosse

Returned to coaching after a successful career in professional rugby. Brings international experience and modern techniques while maintaining the team's core values and fighting spirit.

Dave Thompson

Years Coached: 1995-2004
Hometown: Winona, MN
College: St. Cloud State University

One of the longest-serving coaches in program history. Established many of the traditions and training methods that continue today. Known for his tactical expertise and ability to develop young talent.

Tom Anderson

Years Coached: 1989-1994
Hometown: St. Paul, MN
College: Carleton College

Founding coach of the WSU Men's Rugby program. Established the team's culture and competitive spirit from day one. His vision and leadership laid the groundwork for decades of success.

Winona State Men's Rugby

Established in 1989, the Winona State Men's Rugby Club has been a powerhouse in collegiate rugby.
