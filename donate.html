Why Your Support Matters

As a club sport, the Winona State Men's Rugby team relies heavily on the generosity of our supporters to fund our operations, equipment, travel, and competitive opportunities.

Your donation directly impacts our ability to provide a quality rugby experience for our student-athletes, maintain and improve our facilities, and compete at the highest level of collegiate rugby.
